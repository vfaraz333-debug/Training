Problem Statement

Renewable projects often stall at the interconnection stage due to incomplete or non-compliant transmission planning studies. This training equips engineers to perform PSSE-36 grid connection studies aligned with CEA’s planning philosophy, contingency criteria, and planning procedures introduced in Amendment-I (2025).

2. Technical Learning Objectives

  • Apply the CEA planning philosophy and transmission planning approach to renewable interconnection cases.
  • Build and validate PSSE 36 base cases for grid connection-level studies using CEA-aligned assumptions.
  • Run contingency analysis consistent with CEA criteria and interpret impacts on voltages and loadings.
  • Evaluate substation criteria and additional criteria relevant to renewable integration studies.
  • Follow the CEA planning procedure (Chapter 7) and prepare a concise study summary for submissions.
